title: "Tac2Real: Reliable and GPU Visuotactile Simulation for Online Reinforcement Learning and Zero-Shot Real-World Deployment"
collection: publications
permalink: /publication/Tac2Real
excerpt: 'This paper presents a lightweight simulation framework for training robotic policies with tactile feedback. The system combines the PNCG-IPC contact method with a multi-GPU parallel architecture to achieve interactive simulation speeds. We introduce TacAlign, a technique that addresses both systematic and random sources of discrepancy between simulated and real environments. Testing on a peg insertion task demonstrates successful zero-shot transfer to physical robots, indicating the framework''s robustness and practical applicability.'
