excerpt: 'SIM1 addresses a critical bottleneck in robotic manipulation: generating sufficient training data for cloth-folding tasks. The system bridges the simulation-to-reality gap through three key innovations: (1) Geometric fidelity via laser-scanned garments that create precise digital twins preserving texture and wrinkles; (2) Physics accuracy through a custom deformation-stable solver ensuring cloth behaves identically in simulation and reality; (3) Behavioral alignment using diffusion models to generate human-like trajectories from teleoperated demonstrations. From just 200 human demonstrations, SIM1 generates over 10,000 synthetic trajectories at substantially lower cost. Policies trained entirely on synthetic data achieve 90% zero-shot success on real robots while outperforming real-data baselines by over 50% under environmental changes.'
